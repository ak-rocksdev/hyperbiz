## About HyperBiz

HyperBiz is a web application designed to streamline and simplify the management of key company operations, including transactions, financial management and reports, Simple HRIS (Human Resource Information Systems), and various business activities. The platform aims to improve the efficiency and productivity of organizations by centralizing essential tools in one interface, making management tasks faster and more organized.

## Features

- **Transaction Management**: Track and manage all business transactions in one place.
- **Operational Tools**: Optimize and monitor various internal company processes.
- **Financial Recording and Reporting**: Maintain accurate financial records and generate detailed reports for informed decision-making.
- **HRIS Integration**: Handle employee records, attendance, payroll, and other HR tasks.
- **Accounting Features (Coming Soon)**: Advanced accounting tools to further enhance financial management capabilities.
